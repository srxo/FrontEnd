Attack On Worms

A few hundered years ago there was no internet nor computers. The only storage for all the information were books and our brains. Till today scientist are still researching history and not without reason. Many religions predicted: There will come an apocalypse. And so there came an apocalypse. Worms came out of the ground and the whole world started to shake.                                                                                                                                                                                                                              People ran and tried to escape but only you survived. Giant worms emerged in Egypt and directly went for the Alexandria Library. So much information lost that we as humans lost tons of knowledge including knowledge about geography. Only you can save the world by out smarting all Giant Worms and defeating them with your geography knowledge.

About

We as a team have set a goal to create an educational game for "HAVO 2" students. The game will be based on the curiculum geography.                                                                                                                                                                                                          After having spoken to our target group we found out that these students would like to have a fun way of learning for their tests, we spoke about the students their interests and plan on implying their wishes into the game. This way we hope to make the ciriculum geography more interesting for the students, with higher test results as a result.
